Description

This is an aggressive inline grind trick randomizer. A grind trick is split into 5 parts and each part becomes a reel in the slot machine. Features + Slot machine interface + 20'000+ unique grind tricks + Tricktionary + Trick list editor + Works offline, optimized for smartphones

How to play


Press play and the grind trick slot machine generates a new random trick.
After spinning the reels, you can press a reel to lock it. Press again to disable. Press again to enable.
If you disable a reel, the trick name changes.
If you lock a reel, the reel remaines unchanged when you start a new spin.
If you are happy with the result you can add the trick to a trick list.

Notes

This is a web app for aggressive inline grind tricks. You can use it to build a personal, randomized trick list.
The app uses a slot machine inspired interface and a rule based generator to produce the tricks.
A grind trick is split into approach, spin to grind, grind, grind variation and spin off grind.


I do not aim to define how all the tricks should be named, this should be done by bladers that can actually do them. 😅
Big thanks to Martin, Dean, Kris and all bladers that helped me out a lot with testing, feedback and patience for my trick name questions. 🙏
It was amazing for me to learn how variable grind tricks are. I love this sport. 💗

Enjoy!

Terms

Term Definition
FS Frontside
BS Backside
Approach / Switch Forwards is the opposite of Fakie, Natural is the opposite of Switch. To save screen space Approach and Switch are combined into one slot.
Soul grinds Soul frame based grind wihtout a frontside or a backside variant.
Groove/Boot grinds H-Block based grind with a frontside and a backside variant.
Inspin Spin towards obstacle. If the obstacle is on the left and approach is forwards, Inspin is counter-clockwise. If the obstacle is on the right, Inspin is clockwise.
Outspin Spin away from obstacle, "blindside". If the obstacle is on the left and approach is forwards, Outspin is clockwise. If the obstacle is on the right, Outspin is counter-clockwise.
AO / Alley-oop Forwards to 180 Inspin to a Soul grind
True / Truespin Forwards to 180 Outspin to a Soul grind
Hurricane Forwards to 360 Outspin to a Soul grind
Halfcab Fakie to 180 Inspin to a Soul grind
Fullcab Fakie to 360 Inspin to a Soul grind
True Halfcab Fakie to 180 Outspin to a Soul grind
True Fullcab Fakie to 360 Outspin to a Soul grind
Zerospin Fakie to a Soul grind, no rotation
270 360 spin to a Groove grind. The shorter way (360 - 90 degrees). Note: Groove grinds always are +90 or -90 degrees because the grind is sideways and requires at least a 90 degree spin. There is no 360 Royale, its either 270 or 450 depending on frontside/backside variant, spin direction (inspin, outspin) and approach (fakie/forwards).
450 360 spin to a Groove grind. The longer way (360 + 90 degrees).
Revert / Rewind Spinning off a grind the opposite direction of the natural momentum set by the initial grind spin.

Not implemented

Term Definition
Medspin www.toxboe.net: A 360 done on-ground where you go forward to backwards on foot, then back to forwards on both feet.
Illusion Spin www.enacademic.com: Looking over the opposite shoulder of the direction you are going to spin.
Tough & Rough "Duck Hunt", a Rough and Tough X-Grind.
Step skateyeg.com
Toe/Heel Rolls Before and after the grind.
Negative Topside Exists but I do not understand it at all.
Grabbing locked feet Grabs are only considered as grabbing the free foot of a one legged grind like Makio. A locked feet grab means holding a locked skate, for example a grabbed Full Torque.


Naming debates

Term Definition
The "Savannah" discussion This app goes with the solution proposed by skateyeg.com. I opt for Savannah/Unity instead of AO Unity/Unity. As pointed out by Butch Lehman. Unity is a groove/boot grind. Groove grinds usually do not use the term Alley-oop. I assume most would agree there is no AO Royale. A Royale grinded reverse is a Full Torque, a Torque grinded reverse is a Backslide. This is usually not the case for souls, an AO Soul ist still a Soul, an AO Acid still an Acid. "Usually": both souls and grooves have exceptions though, some souls have a different name when grinded reverse Soyale (AO Torque Soul), Misfit (AO Top Mistrial), Kindgrind (AO Top Mizou) and some grooves do not have another name when grinded reverse (=> "True Tabernacle Discussion").
The "True Tabernacle" discussion Tricks like Tabernacle, Wheelbarrow, Darkslide, Fastslide, Byn Soul, Neighborhood or Acid Rain are hybrids between Groove and Soul grinds. They are "h-block tricks that are done like AO/Truespin.". They are hybrids because they have a BS and FS variant as every Groove grind but also have an Alley-Oop variant when grinded reverse as every Soul grind (=> The "Savannah" discussion). This app solves this by treating these hybrids as "FS" and "BS" Soul tricks. Therefore there is no 'True Tabernacle' but a 'True BS Tabernacle' or 'True FS Tabernacle'.
